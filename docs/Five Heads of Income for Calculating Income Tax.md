# Five Heads of Income for [[Calculating Income Tax]]

- Income from salaries
- Income from Capital Gains
    The gain earned from selling property, shares, mutual funds etc.
- Income from Business/Profession
    If you own a business, shop etc. or you are a doctor, CA or some other kind of freelancer or professional.
- Income from house property (rentals)
    If you've rented out a property and you receive a regular income from that. In fact, this can be negative as well. For example, you just pay interest on that property and its not rented, then it counts as negative income. This can be used to save taxes.
- Income from any other source
